How to Send Fax via E-Mail

To send a fax using our Email-to-Fax service simply send an email using the parameters provided below. Email-to-Fax supports up to 5 attachments. Attachments may be sent in JPG, PDF, Adobe PostScript, TIFF, Microsoft Word, Excel, CSV, HTML & Plain Text formats.

Content put in the body of the email will be included on a cover page.

You may only send faxes from the email address associated with your Bridge account. Upon submission our system will reply with a confirmation or rejection notice.
